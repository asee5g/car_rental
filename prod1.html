⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The 2022 Hyundai Tucson received a five-star safety rating from the European New Car Assessment Programme (Euro NCAP). The Tucson performed well in the following categories: Adult occupant, Child occupant, and Safety assist.

The Tucson's safety package includes:

● Highway Driving Assist (HDA)

● Blind-spot View Monitor (BVM)

● Blind-spot Collision-avoidance Assist (BCA)

● Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist (FCA)

Euro NCAP performs a series of crash and safety tests on car models, including frontal impact tests.
